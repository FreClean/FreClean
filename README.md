## Overview

FreClean is a professional cleaning initiative dedicated to improving hygiene, health, and environmental sustainability through high-quality cleaning services, trusted cleaning products, and entrepreneurship opportunities.

Our mission is to make professional cleaning solutions accessible while creating economic opportunities for individuals and communities.

FreClean combines innovation, education, and sustainable business practices to build cleaner homes, healthier workplaces, and stronger communities.

---

# Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and environmentally responsible cleaning products and services while empowering people through entrepreneurship, training, and innovation.

---

# Vision

To become one of the most trusted cleaning brands in the Caribbean and beyond by delivering exceptional quality, customer satisfaction, and sustainable impact.
